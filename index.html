About Dr. Sood

Dr. Sood with His Holiness the Dalai Lama
Dr. Sood with Senator Elizabeth Dole

Dr. Amit Sood is one of the world's leading experts on resiliency and wellbeing. He is also the creator of the Resilient Option program and is the Executive Director of the Global Center for Resiliency & Wellbeing.

Dr. Sood is a former Professor and Chair for student life & wellness at the Mayo Clinic and currently serves on the Wellness Advisory Board for Everyday Health. He is also an RWJ Foundation Health Care Pioneer

He has received numerous awards at the Mayo Clinic including the 2010 Distinguished Service, 2010 Innovator of the Year, 2013 Outstanding Physician Scientist, and 2016 Faculty of the Year.

Dr. Sood was named as one among top 20 intelligent optimists helping the world be a better place by Ode Magazine. He has also been a speaker at several prestigious forums and organizations including TEDx, Lake Nona, Forbes Under 30 Forum, Conference Board, Beckers, YPO, NPR, NAMI, NBGH, NASA, among other.
